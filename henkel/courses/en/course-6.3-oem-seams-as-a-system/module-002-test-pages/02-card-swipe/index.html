Humidity horrors

Adhesives can be adversely affected by moisture during the curing process, causing bubbles, dilution or unwanted films.

Water vapour can penetrate some substrates and deteriorate the bond strength.

Water worries

Water can swell certain adhesives and affect the strength.

Water/moisture can react with some adhesives, changing their properties.

So what's the solution?

The use of primers and surface preparation can help prevent degradation.

Ensure you use the right adhesive for the specific requirements of the application.
Epoxies and silicones: GOOD
Cyanoacrylates: BAD
