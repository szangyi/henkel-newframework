Force File #1

Vibration

A force to be reckoned with.

Vibration can severely impact the performance and durability of many joining methods, and repeated or continuous exposure can weaken your substrates.

When:

7th November, 1940

What:

The Tacoma Narrows Bridge in Washington, USA, is designed to be the most flexible bridge ever constructed. Spoiler alert: it doesn’t work...

Why:

High winds hit the area, tossing the bridge wildly until it breaks apart (luckily not before everyone had escaped their cars). Investigations reveal that the bridge was vulnerable to vibrations generated by wind. When exposed to strong winds from a particular direction, the frequency oscillations kept building... until collapse was inevitable.
